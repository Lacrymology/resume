Choose "Custom Away...", then enter the following EXACTLY:
### %Track - %Artist

Now I can work and share my playlist, while discouraging social distractions. My friends and colleagues know when it comes to my chat status:

### Red light *STOP*. Green light *GO*.

The technique works for me using iChat 5.0.1 on Snow Leopard 10.6.2. [ YMMV](http://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/your_mileage_may_vary). via [jca488 @ macrumors](http://forums.macrumors.com/showthread.php?t=589418#post6725775).
